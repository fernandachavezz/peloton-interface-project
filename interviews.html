User Interviews

👤 User #1: Casual Peloton Rider

Experience Level: Uses the Peloton twice a week for the past three months.

🚴 Typical Routine

  • Logs in and selects "Just Ride"
  • Rides for **10 miles** without taking structured classes

⚠️ Challenges

  • Never connected **headphones** before.
  • Didn't realize Peloton had **structured classes**.

💡 Suggested Improvements

  • 🔍 **Feature Discovery:** Add a tutorial pop-up for first-time users.
  • 🎧 **One-click Bluetooth setup** for headphones.
  • ⚙️ **Saved User Preferences:** Auto-save seat height and resistance settings.

👤 User #2: Experienced but Infrequent User

Experience Level: Used to ride **every day**, now uses it **occasionally**.

🎯 Expectations Before First Ride

Initially thought it was just a **regular stationary bike**—was surprised to find instructors and **music-matching workouts**.

🚴 Typical Routine

  • Logs into account.
  • Searches for a specific **instructor** based on **playlist preferences**.
  • Connects **headphones** and starts the ride.

🖥️ Navigation Experience

Finds it **self-explanatory** and intuitive. Appreciates the **intro guide when signing up**.

⚠️ Challenges

  • Occasional **Bluetooth connection issues** with headphones.
  • Struggles to **adjust the seat properly**.

⭐ Favorite Features

  • Metrics keep them accountable—especially **leaderboards and calorie tracking**.
  • Gamification and **digital experience** make it feel better than **traditional gym equipment**.

💡 Suggested Improvements

  • 🔍 **Easier Bike Adjustments:** More intuitive seat and handlebar customization.
  • 🎧 **Simpler Bluetooth Pairing:** A **one-click setup** when starting a workout.

👤 User #3: First-Time Peloton User

Experience Level: New to Peloton, has used standard stationary bikes before.

🎯 Expectations Before First Ride

Thought it would be a **basic stationary bike**, surprised by the **digital interface and instructor-led classes**.

🚴 Typical Routine

  • Logs in and browses class options.
  • Filters by **duration and music genre**.
  • Starts a **20-30 minute beginner-friendly ride**.

🖥️ Navigation Experience

Search function is **easy to use**, but took time to understand **leaderboard and performance tracking**.

⚠️ Challenges

  • Had trouble adjusting the **seat height**.
  • Found too many **class options overwhelming**.
  • Didn’t immediately understand **output metrics** like cadence.

⭐ Favorite Features

  • Instructor-led classes with **music integration**.
  • Leaderboards add **a fun competitive aspect**.
  • Cool-down and **post-workout stats** were useful.

💡 Suggested Improvements

  • 💡 **Pre-ride tutorial** for new users.
  • 🚴 **Quick-start button** for users who just want to ride.
  • 🔧 **More intuitive bike adjustments** before starting a workout.

🚀 Final Thoughts

Excited to explore more classes, would **recommend Peloton to beginners**.
