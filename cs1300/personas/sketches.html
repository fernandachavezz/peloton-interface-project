Peloton Sketches

What is a Peloton?

The Peloton is a high-tech stationary bike designed for interactive workouts. It features a large touchscreen, allowing users to take live and on-demand fitness classes from home.

How Does It Work?

Peloton bikes provide an immersive workout experience by offering real-time performance tracking, leaderboard rankings, and a variety of instructor-led classes. Users can adjust resistance levels, track metrics like cadence and output, and even connect to Bluetooth headphones for a seamless experience.

Annotated Sketch of the Peloton Bike

Key Components:

  • Screen: Displays live and recorded classes.
  • Seat & Seat Adjuster: Customizable for different users.
  • Resistance Adjuster: Allows users to control workout intensity.
  • Pedals: Designed for cycling shoes with clip-in support.
  • Handlebars: Ergonomic grips for comfort during workouts.
  • Water Bottle Holder: Provides easy access to hydration.
